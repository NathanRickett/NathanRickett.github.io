Understanding the Risk Management Framework (RMF)

A foundational approach to securing information systems

What is RMF?

The Risk Management Framework (RMF), developed by NIST, provides a structured process for integrating security and risk management activities into the system development life cycle. It is widely used across federal agencies and contractors to ensure systems meet rigorous security standards.
