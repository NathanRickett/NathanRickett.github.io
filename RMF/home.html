Welcome to the Risk Management Framework (RMF) Project

This site is your interactive guide through the NIST Risk Management Framework (RMF), designed to help you understand and apply each step of the process in a practical, hands-on way.

Start by selecting a phase from the navigation menu on the left. Each section includes detailed explanations, lab simulations, and documentation examples to help reinforce your learning.

Whether you're preparing for a cybersecurity role, working on a government contract, or building your own home lab, this resource is tailored to give you both theoretical knowledge and real-world application.
